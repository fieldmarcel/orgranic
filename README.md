
🍽️ Organic - The Multi User Food Recipe Website

Organic is a specially curated Indian food recipe website where you can explore a wide variety of authentic recipes from different regions of India. Whether it's the rich flavors of North Indian curries, the coastal delights of South India, or the street food magic of Mumbai, Organic brings delicious and traditional recipes right to your fingertips.

🚀 Features
📝 User Contributions & their comments-replies functionality: Add your own unique recipes to the collection.

🌍 Regional Recipes: Explore recipes from various regions of India.

📖 Step-by-Step Instructions: Easy-to-follow recipe steps.

🍛 Diverse Cuisine Options: From spicy curries to tasty desserts.

🔍 Search Functionality: Quickly find recipes based on ingredients or cuisine.

💾 Save Your Favorites: Bookmark your preferred recipes.


🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: React, Tailwind CSS,React-Redux


Backend: Node.js, Express.js

Database: MongoDB
UI Libraries- ShadCN ui , Aceternityui 
Animations- Framer Motion
